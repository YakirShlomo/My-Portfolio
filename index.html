About Me

Hello, I'm Yakir Shlomo, a second-year software engineering student at SCE College. My passion for computers began as a child, always curious about how websites, games, and technologies were created. I often wondered if I could build these things myself, and that curiosity drove me to learn and experiment independently.

This website showcases the projects I've developed so far, providing a glimpse into my passion for development. I am driven by the desire to deepen my knowledge, enhance my skills, and bring my ideas to fruition.

My goals are to grow into a skilled Full Stack Developer capable of creating responsive and efficient web applications, as well as becoming a creative Game Developer who designs immersive and engaging gaming experiences. I see these two fields as a perfect combination of creativity and planning, and I strive to excel in both while finding ways to integrate them seamlessly.

I believe that if you like a certain field of interest, go with it as far as possible and continuously develop your skills, thinking, and abilities. Therefore, I'm always looking for ways to improve and know as much as possible. "Knowledge is power."

If you're looking for someone dedicated, creative, and passionate about the profession, take a look through my projects, and let's get in touch!
